## [v0.2.1] - Wed, May 27

* `co` will now expand to *color* instead of *content*.
* Added *visibility*, *zoom* and *clear* shortcuts.

## [v0.2.0] - May 27, 2015

* `margin: 3⏎` will now expand to `margin: 3px;` (and with similar properties too).
* Added `abs⏎` and `rel⏎`.
* Added `ts:` for *text-shadow*.
